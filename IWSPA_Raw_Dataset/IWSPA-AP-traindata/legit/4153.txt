Live: Oscar nominations set to begin
Los Angeles Times | January 16, 2014 | 5:29 AM
Nominations for the 86th Academy Awards are scheduled to begin at 5:38 a.m. Pacific time today at the Samuel Goldwyn Theater in Beverly Hills. "American Hustle," "12 Years a Slave" and "Gravity" are among the films considered to be locks for best picture nominations.
