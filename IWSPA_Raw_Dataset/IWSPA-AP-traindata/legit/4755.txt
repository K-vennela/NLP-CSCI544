Breaking News: 'Hustle,' '12 Years a Slave' among PGA Award nominees
Los Angeles Times | January 2, 2014 | 9:55 AM
The Producers Guild of America today nominated the producers of 10 films, including "American Hustle," "12 Years a Slave," "Nebraska" and "Gravity," for the Darryl F. Zanuck Producer of the Year Award for theatrical motion pictures.
