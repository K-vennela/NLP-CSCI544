'300: Rise of an Empire' tops 'Mr. Peabody' at box office
Los Angeles Times | March 9, 2014 | 12:07 PM
'Rise of an Empire," a 3-D sequel to the 2007 movie "300," grossed an estimated $45.1 million in ticket sales across the U.S. and Canada to take the No. 1 spot at the box office while "Mr. Peabody," a Dreamworks Animation movie that was released by 20th Century Fox, grossed an estimated $32.5 million domestically on its opening weekend to take No. 2.
