Breaking News: Actor Efrem Zimbalist Jr., '77 Sunset Strip' star, dies
Los Angeles Times | May 2, 2014 | 10:19 PM
Efrem Zimbalist Jr., who costarred as the suave private eye on TV's "77 Sunset Strip," has died. He was 95.
Zimbalist, the father of actress Stephanie Zimbalist, died Friday at home in Solvang.
