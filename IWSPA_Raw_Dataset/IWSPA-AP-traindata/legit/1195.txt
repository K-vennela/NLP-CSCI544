Breaking: Cate Blanchett wins best actress Oscar
Los Angeles Times | March 2, 2014 | 8:44 PM
Cate Blanchett won the Oscar for lead actress at Sunday's 86th Academy Awards for her portrayal of Jasmine, the fallen New York socialite in the Woody Allen film "Blue Jasmine." Blanchett's Oscar, her second, was largely expected, as the actress had swept all awards in that category leading up to the Oscars.
