OutKast, Muse, Arcade Fire, the Replacements to anchor Coachella
Los Angeles Times | January 8, 2014 | 8:02 PM
A reunited OutKast will anchor the 2014 edition of the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival. The Grammy-winning hip-hop act last released an album in 1996. The rap duo will be joined by indie rockers Arcade Fire, British act Muse and a reunited Replacements.
Other acts at the top of the bill for the dual Coachella festivals, set for the weekends of April 12 and April 19 on a polo field in the desert city of Indio, include Queens of the Stone Age, Haim, Skrillex and Beck.
Remaining tickets for the two weekends will go on sale Jan. 10 via the official Coachella site. The bulk of tickets were sold via a pre-sale that began in May. A three-day pass starts at $349.
