Stephen Colbert to replace David Letterman as 'Late Show' host
Los Angeles Times | April 10, 2014 | 9:24 AM
Stephen Colbert will replace David Letterman as the host of "The Late Show" when Letterman retires next year, CBS announced today.
The network said it had reached a five-year agreement with Colbert, the star of Comedy Central's "The Colbert Report."
Producers and the location for the Colbert-hosted "Late Show" will be determined at a later date, the network said.
