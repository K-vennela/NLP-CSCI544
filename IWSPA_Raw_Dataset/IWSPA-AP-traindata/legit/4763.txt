Box Office: 'Frozen,' 'Catching Fire' set Thanksgiving-weekend records
Los Angeles Times | December 1, 2013 | 10:58 AM
The holiday box office exploded this weekend as both "The Hunger Games: Catching Fire" and "Frozen" topped records held by "Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone" for the biggest three-day and five-day Thanksgiving weekend grosses. "Catching Fire," in its second weekend, earned $110.2 million in five days, while "Frozen" scored $93 million over the longer Thanksgiving holiday.
