Box Office: 'Spider-Man' can't hang with 'Neighbors'
Los Angeles Times | May 11, 2014 | 9:43 AM
R-rated comedy "Neighbors" far exceeded industry expectations at the domestic box office this weekend, debuting with $51.1 million, according to an estimate from Universal. "The Amazing Spider-Man 2" saw its ticket sales tumble 59% to $37.2 million in its second weekend in release.
