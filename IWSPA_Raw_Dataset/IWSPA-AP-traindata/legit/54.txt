Tony Awards: Best play is named
Los Angeles Times | June 8, 2014 | 6:50 PM
Robert Schenkkan's Lyndon B. Johnson tale "All The Way" was named best play at the 68th Tony Awards, presented Sunday night at Radio City Music Hall.
