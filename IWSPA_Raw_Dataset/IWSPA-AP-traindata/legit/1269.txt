Box Office: 'Peabody,' '300' beat 'Need for Speed'; 'Single Moms Club' weak
Los Angeles Times | March 16, 2014 | 10:51 AM
DreamWorks Animation's "Mr. Peabody & Sherman" scored a box-office victory with a strong second weekend of an estimated $21.2 million in ticket sales. "300: Rise of an Empire" was No. 2 with $19.1 million while "Need for Speed" fell short of expectations with $17.8 million.
