Sid Caesar, TV comedy pioneer, dies at 91
Los Angeles Times | February 12, 2014 | 12:19 PM
Sid Caesar, a television pioneer who reigned as the king of live TV sketch comedy in the 1950s with his inspired brand of mimicry, pantomime and satire on the classic comedy-variety series "Your Show of Shows" and "Caesar's Hour," died today. He was 91.
