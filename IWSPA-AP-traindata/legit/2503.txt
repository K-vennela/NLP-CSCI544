Tony Awards: Best musical is named
Los Angeles Times | June 8, 2014 | 8:04 PM
Robert L. Freedman and Steven Lutvak's death-happy comedy "A Gentleman's Guide to Love & Murder" was named best musical at the 68th Tony Awards, presented Sunday night at Radio City Music Hall.
