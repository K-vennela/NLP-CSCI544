Box Office: 'Divergent' delivers, Muppets stumble
Los Angeles Times | March 23, 2014 | 1:01 PM
Any talk of a curse on young-adult film franchises was put on hold this weekend with the estimated $56-million opening for "Divergent," the first adaptation from a series of novels by Veronica Roth, while "Muppets Most Wanted" underperformed with $16.5 million and "God's Not Dead" surprised some with a strong turnout from faith-based audiences.
