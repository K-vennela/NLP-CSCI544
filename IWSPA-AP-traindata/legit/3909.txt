Box Office: 'Captain America' edges out 'Rio 2' for No. 1
Los Angeles Times | April 13, 2014 | 10:30 AM
"Captain America: The Winter Soldier" retained its superhero grip at the weekend box office finishing No. 1 with a studio-estimated $41.4 million in its second weekend and beating the animated "Rio 2," which took in $39 million.
