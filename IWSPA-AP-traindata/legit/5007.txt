Box Office: 'Lucy' outmuscles 'Hercules'
Los Angeles Times | July 27, 2014 | 11:50 AM
Universal's "Lucy" led the multiplex after it took in an impressive $44 million in its first weekend, according to studio estimates. That performance was more than enough to trump Paramount Pictures and Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer Pictures' "Hercules," which took in $29 million over the weekend.
