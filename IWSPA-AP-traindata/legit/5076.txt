Box Office: 'Spider-Man 2' opens big, but falls short of 'Captain America' record
Los Angeles Times | May 4, 2014 | 9:38 AM
"The Amazing Spider-Man 2," which hit theaters Thursday evening, debuted with $92 million, falling short of having the biggest opening of 2014 -- which is owned by fellow superhero film "Captain America: The Winter Soldier."
