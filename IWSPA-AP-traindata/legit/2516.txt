Box Office: 'Captain America' jilted for 'The Other Woman'
Los Angeles Times | April 27, 2014 | 9:23 AM
"The Other Woman" knocked "Captain America: The Winter Soldier" out of the top box office spot with a healthy $24.7 million, according to an estimate from distributor 20th Century Fox. After coming in at No. 1 for three consecutive weekends, the "Captain America" sequel had to settle for the runner-up position with $16 million in sales.
