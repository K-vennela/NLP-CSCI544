'Breaking Bad' and 'Modern Family' win SAG television ensemble awards
Los Angeles Times | January 18, 2014 | 6:47 PM
"Breaking Bad" won the ensemble prize for television drama series and "Modern Family" took the ensemble award for comedy at the 20th Screen Actors Guild Awards on Saturday.
