Maya Angelou dies at 86
Los Angeles Times | May 28, 2014 | 6:58 AM
Maya Angelou, the poet and actress best known for her 1970 memoir "I Know Why the Caged Bird Sings," died today. She was 86.
She died at her home in Winston-Salem, N.C., and her death was announced by Wake Forest University.
