Bernie Sanders has won the Oregon Democratic primary, according to The Associated Press.
Sanders trailed Hillary Clinton in recent polling conducted in the state, though the Vermont senator's campaign had long been bullish on his chances in the Pacific Northwest given his strong performance in the Washington state caucus.
Oregon awards its 61 pledged delegates on a proportional basis.
