Los Angeles Times Entertainment Breaking News 
Updated: Emmy nominations announced
Los Angeles Times | July 10, 2014 | 6:09 AM
"Breaking Bad," "Downton Abbey," "Game of Thrones," "House of Cards," "Mad Men," and "True Detective" received nominations for outstanding drama series at the 66th Primetime Emmy Awards this morning. Meanwhile, "The Big Bang Theory," "Louie," "Modern Family," "Orange is the New Black," "Silicon Valley" and "Veep" received nominations for outstanding comedy.
A previous breaking news alert said "Homeland" was nominated for drama series. It was not.
