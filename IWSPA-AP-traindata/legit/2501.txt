Hillary Clinton claimed victory in the Kentucky Democratic primary on Tuesday night, though The Associated Press said the race was too close to call with 99 percent of precincts reporting. 
"We just won Kentucky! Thanks to everyone who turned out. We're always stronger united," Clinton tweeted.
With only a handful of precincts yet to report, Clinton had edged ahead of Bernie Sanders, 46.7 percent to 46.3 percent.
